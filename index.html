What is this site for?

This site is a showcase of my apps. I have only two as of now but I'm constantly working so expect consistent updates and new products. For now though, you will see my Translator app, appropriately named Translator and my Bluetooth Controller app. This apps are in a state near enough to completion that I would deem them not only useable but useful too. These are non profit apps made with the MIT App Inventor software which is a free and easy way of getting into coding and is a gentle introduction to code. For now my apps can be gotten directly from the MIT App Inventor gallery where I have published them. Hopefully you may see them on Google Play soon.
